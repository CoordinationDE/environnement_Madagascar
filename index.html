Interventionzones of Development Projects in the Environmental Sector of Madagascar

About This Website

This interactive map was created to visualize the intervention zones of different development projects working in the same geographical and thematic context. Its goal is to help identify synergies and overlaps, and ultimately increase the collective impact of individual projects.

The data displayed has been provided by the respective projects and other relevant sources, which are cited below where applicable:
